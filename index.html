Privacy Policy

dotRead does not collect any data. It has no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and makes no network connections.

The app only reads and writes the Markdown files you explicitly open or create. Those files stay on your Mac (and in any iCloud/Drive folders you keep them in) — they are never sent anywhere by dotRead.

Because dotRead collects nothing, there is nothing to share, sell, or delete. If this policy ever changes, it will be updated on this page.
